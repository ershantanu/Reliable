Temperature Measuring Instrument– testo 735




Description:

The multichannel temperature measuring instrument testo 735 can measure the surface, air and core temperature of a whole range of media and thus can be used for measuring temperature distribution in air conditioning cabinets and refrigerators, etc.

 

USPs:

• Highest precision over the entire measuring range thanks to system adjustment

• System accuracy up to 0.05 °C

• Display, storage and print-out of Delta T, min., max. and mean values

• Audible alarm (adjustable limit values)

• Cyclic printing of measurement values, e.g once per minute

• Protection class IP65

• Certified according to EN 13485

 

Long Description:

"The testo 735-1 temperature measuring instrument can deal with almost all measuring tasks. That is because the comprehensive selection of optional probes enables reliable temperature measurement in a variety of areas. This means you can for instance use the measuring instrument in chemical laboratories for temperature measurement in aggressive media, for measuring temperature distribution in air conditioning cabinets and refrigerators, for calibrating stationary temperature probes or in the food industry for measuring core temperature. Measure surface, air and core temperature – in up to 6 locations at the same time Whether your work involves core temperatures, surface temperatures or air temperatures – choose the temperature probes that are appropriate for you from our large selection of probes and measure the temperature in up to six locations at the same time with the testo 735-1 temperature measuring device. The temperature measuring instrument has one probe input for attaching high-precision Pt100 probes and two inputs for attaching fast thermocouple probes of the K, T, J and S types. Take advantage of wireless measurement data transfer by radio as well. The optional radio module means you can receive readings from up to three further optional radio temperature probes on the testo 735-1. The radio probes send the temperature values to the temperature measuring instrument over a distance of up to 20 meters where there are no obstructions. Wireless measurement enables you to avoid damage to cables or hindrances to handling. Accurate temperature measurement with Pt100 temperature probe Pt100 technology enables you to achieve very accurate temperature measurements with the testo 735-1. The high-precision Pt100 immersion/penetration probe (optional) thus achieves system accuracy of up to 0.05 °C with a resolution of 0.001 °C. The temperature measuring instrument is therefore ideally suited to use as a working standard for calibration of stationary temperature probes. The testo 735-1 temperature measuring instrument offers great features and standard-compliant work The testo 735-1 temperature measuring device does not just display the temperatures currently being measured. Min./max. and mean values are also shown on the instrument display. When two temperature probes are attached, the temperature measuring instrument calculates the differential temperature and displays this as delta T. In addition, an acoustic alarm sounds when limit values are undershot or exceeded. You are free to define the limit values. The display illumination also enables easy reading of measuring values even in dark surroundings. In addition, the testo 735-1 temperature measuring instrument is waterproof according to protection class IP 65, has been tested in accordance with EN 13485, is HACCP-compliant and has international HACCP certification. of measurement data on site via the testo fast printer If you need evidence of the measured temperature values, we offer our optional testo fast printer as an accessory for the temperature measuring instrument. You can use this to print out the current temperature values or the min./max. values on site. Use the practical cyclical printing function on the testo 735-1 as well. It not only enables the printout of individual readings, but also of whole series of measurements on the testo fast printer at freely adjustable intervals (for example once a minute). We recommend you use the testo 735-2 version for saving, documenting and archiving measurement data. This temperature measuring instrument also has a reading memory and PC software."
