testo 416 - Air flow meter with a telescopic vane




Description:

 

Product Description

 

The testo 416 air flow meter provides you with quick and reliable air flow readings on an easy-to-read, backlit display. The permanently connected telescopic sensor, which can be extended to a maximum length of 890 mm and a mini vane that has a diameter of 16 mm make the air flow meter ideal for measuring the air flow and air volume in air ducts. In order to calculate the air volume, simply enter the duct cross section area into the air flow meter. The timed and point mean value calculations will then show the average air volume. The testo 416 air flow meter also has a hold function which allows you to freeze the last reading as well as min/max readings which can be displayed at the touch of a key.


More accessories for even more benefits in the field

Additional optional accessories include a practical all-in-one shoulder bag or carrying case and a TopSafe protective case that protects the air flow meter against impact, dirt and water.
